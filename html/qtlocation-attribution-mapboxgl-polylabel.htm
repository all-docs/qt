polylabel, version 1.0.3

A fast algorithm for finding polygon pole of inaccessibility.

Used in the Mapbox GL plugin of Qt Location.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtlocation/src/3rdparty/mapbox-gl-native/deps/polylabel.
