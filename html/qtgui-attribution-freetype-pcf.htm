Freetype 2 - Portable Compiled Format (PCF) support

FreeType is a freely available software library to render fonts.

Optionally used in Qt GUI and platform plugins. Configure with -no-freetype, or -system-freetype to avoid.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/freetype/src/pcf.
